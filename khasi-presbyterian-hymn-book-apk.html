Khasi Presbyterian Hymn Book apk/ Ka Kot Jingrwai Khasi app

Ka Kot Jingrwai app ka dei ka kot jingrwai Presbyterian. Ia kane ka app la pyllait da ka jingthmu ba khnang ba ki pait riewngeit ki lah ban pyndonkam hangno hangno haba ki don ha ki por leit jingleit bad haba donkam kyrkieh bym don kot Jingrwai ha met. Kane ka dei ka version ba nyngkong kumta lah ban don shibun ki jingduna bad jingbakla.
La buh ia ki "Number Jingrwai" bad lah ban shu nion beit ha ki budam lane wad da kaba shu type ia ki Number. La buh ruh ia ka list ha ka "Jingkdew" bad lah ban wad da kaba shu type ia ki Number lane kyrteng jingrwai. Ha ka por ba wad jingrwai, ym donkam ban type ia ki special character. Ha kane ka list, lah ruh ban peit da ki kyrteng shuwa katkum ki Alphabet lane ki Number shuwa katkum ka jingkwah.
